Prenatal & Postnatal Services

In conjunction with the Department of Reproductive Medicine at Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital, comprehensive services are provided to mothers before and after they deliver. In terms of prenatal services, our doctors and nurses work closely with mothers to identify those who are high-risk for complications from pregnancy and whose babies may be at risk as well. "We follow these patients closely and our goal is to prevent and reduce problems for mothers and their babies," Dr. Hillary Mabeya, Chairman of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Moi U. Medical School. "After a mother has delivered, we focus on her well-being as we do that of her baby. We are also a leader in preventing and repairing obstetric fistulas."
