Labor & Delivery

The health of our mothers and their babies is what is most important. "We welcome all women to our state-of-the art labor and delivery suite," states Dr. Haroun Mengech, Director of Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital. "Every woman's labor and delivery experience is unique and we are here to provide support through this process." Although labor and delivery generally follows a predictable pattern, the unexpected can happen. RMBHK has medical specialists who can address all problems – including neonatologist to monitor a baby’s progress or an obstetrician who can perform a C-section in our new facilities. In our family focused program, all babies are welcomed with joy into the world.
